PostDraft — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

About PostDraft

PostDraft is a content scheduling and publishing tool for TikTok slideshow posts. This Privacy Policy explains how PostDraft handles your data.

Data Collection

PostDraft does not collect, store, or share any personal data from TikTok users beyond what is strictly required to provide the scheduling and publishing functionality. PostDraft accesses TikTok's API solely to publish content on behalf of the authenticated account owner.

TikTok API Usage

When you connect your TikTok account, PostDraft accesses the following through TikTok's official OAuth flow:

  • user.info.basic — basic profile information (open ID, avatar, display name)
  • user.info.profile — profile web link, deep link, bio description, verification status
  • user.info.stats — likes count, follower count, following count, video count
  • video.list — read access to your existing public videos
  • video.upload — upload videos and photo slideshows to your account
  • video.publish — publish uploaded content to your account

This data is used exclusively for content scheduling, publishing, and performance tracking inside PostDraft. It is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data Storage

Post content, drafts, and analytics data are stored on the PostDraft account owner's own self-hosted infrastructure. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond TikTok's own API.

Data Retention

PostDraft retains data only as long as the user maintains an active connection to their TikTok account. Disconnecting the TikTok account from PostDraft immediately deletes all stored authentication tokens.

Data Deletion

You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time through the PostDraft settings. Upon disconnection, all stored authentication tokens and personal data are immediately deleted. To request full deletion of any remaining data, contact dorukdumlu@gmail.com.

Third-Party Services

PostDraft only communicates with TikTok's official APIs. No third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services are used.
