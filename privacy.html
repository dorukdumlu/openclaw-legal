Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data Collection

OpenClaw does not collect, store, or share any personal data from TikTok users. The app accesses TikTok's API solely to publish content on behalf of the authenticated account owner.

TikTok API Usage

When you connect your TikTok account, OpenClaw accesses the following through TikTok's official OAuth flow:

  • Basic profile information (username, display name)
  • Ability to publish content (photos, slideshows) to your account
  • Post performance analytics (views, likes, comments)

This data is used exclusively for content scheduling and performance tracking. It is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

Data Storage

Post content and analytics data are stored on the account owner's own self-hosted infrastructure. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond TikTok's own API.

Data Deletion

You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time through the app settings. Upon disconnection, all stored authentication tokens are immediately deleted.
